Bringing strangers together to transform spaces and create immersive surroundings. Live music, film screenings, seminars, exhibitions and workshops in an intimate, magical environment.

The four of us met on our university degree programme, Global Markets, Local Creativities, which took us on a two-year journey through universities in Glasgow, Barcelona, Gottingen and Rotterdam. We learned about globalisation, entrepreneurship and the arts all over the world, and fell in love with Barcelona for its views, architecture and booming arts scene. So much so that we are back.
